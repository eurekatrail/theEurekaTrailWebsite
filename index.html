The Eureka Trail



It's 1850. You and your caravan are hoping to strike it rich by surviving the Westward trek to Eureka, California during the height of the gold rush. Test your wits and fitness by taking steps in the real world, earning money for supplies, hunting for food, tending animals, and avoiding dysentery, thieves, and other trail challenges! Remember, each step you and your caravan members take brings you closer to Eureka where you can stake your claim to Gold and assured fortune

Key Features



Active

Players' real-world steps are integrated into gameplay

Cooperative

Intended for intergenerational families and friends to play together

Narrative

A gold-rush theme that emphasizes both survival and speed

Fun

Snarky and playful tone written to appeal to both children and adults

Begin your Expedition!



Choose a role

You'll begin by choosing one of 10 roles. Each role come with their unique personalities and abilities. The Wagon Master will decide what day the caravan will leave to start the journey west.

Play with friends

It's dangerous to go alone! Invite your friends to join your caravan. Nudge them if they're getting behind.

Don't forget to walk

Survival depends on making it to the next checkpoint. Your caravan's fame and fortune depend on reaching the Eureka boomtown early, so get moving!Â
