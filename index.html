It's 1850.

You and your caravan are hoping to strike it rich by surviving the Westward trek to Eureka, California during the height of the gold rush. Test your wits and fitness by taking steps in the real world, earning money for supplies, hunting for food, tending animals, and avoiding dysentery, thieves, and other trail challenges! Remember, each step you and your caravan members take brings you closer to Eureka where you can stake your claim to Gold and assured fortune.

Choose a role

You'll begin by choosing one of 10 roles. Each role come with their unique personalities and abilities. The Wagon Master will decide what day the caravan will leave to start the journey west.

Play with friends

It's dangerous to go alone! Invite your friends to join your caravan. Nudge them if they're getting behind.
